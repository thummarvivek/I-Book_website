Tourist-Ads.Com is a Travel Ad Network. We Buy and Sell Tourists Traffic World Wide. Both Globally and Locally.

We work with all kinds of tourist attractions, including: Hotels, Car Rentals, Local Restaurants, Spas, Night Clubs and even Cruises! Basically we work with every business that needs to attract tourists.

The idea is to provide tourist attractions with a very targeted specific tourists traffic who are planning to visit their home city. We are also in touch with local travel publishers like travel blogers and tourist guides in order to buy their local traffic and send these tourists straight to the tourist attractions in that specific city or country.

Why do tourist attractions need targeted traffic?

Let's Say you own an hotel in Prague, you would like to attract tourists planning to travel Prague, you would not want to attract tourists traveling to Las Vegas!

We intend to be the Google Adsense of the travel industry, On every location we intend to buy, sell and swap targeted travel traffic, we buy traffic from travel publishers and sell that traffic to travel advertisers or to tourist attractions in the same area.
